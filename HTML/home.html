Meet our Founder

Mr. Bruce

Mr. Bruce founded Medigo, a mobile healthcare platform transforming how people access care in Nigeria. Frustrated by long wait times, rigid systems, and inaccessible services, he launched Medigo to make healthcare as easy and intuitive as booking a ride. With a focus on speed, convenience, and trust, Medigo connects users to licensed doctors anytime, anywhere—whether at home, at work, or on the move. Bruce mission is simple: put healthcare back in the hands of patients and shape a more responsive, mobile-first future for health in Africa.
